---
title: "Enrich Company"
description: "Extract relevant insights about an organization and its leadership"
---

The `Enrich Company` module allows you to enter the company's name and domain to automatically gather detailed insights, including an overview,  ammount of employees, funding rounds, and more.

The `Enrich Company` module has **two inputs** and **variable outputs, **which you can choose in the configurations:

- **Inputs**: The module has to receive the`website domain`of the organization and its name.
- **Output**:
  - `Report`: An extensive report on the organization
  - `Industry`: The industry of the business
  - `Location`: Where the company is located at
  - `Linkedin URL`: Retrieves the company's LinkedIn URL
  - `Number of Employees`: The estimate of how many people are in the company
  - `Funding rounds`: Information on the funding rounds
- **Configs**:
  - `Output as JSON`: If enabled, returns the result as a raw JSON result instead of a formatted markdown
  - `Use Linkedin URL`, the search will be made via Linkedin URL instead of Website domain and name of the company
